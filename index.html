I'm Dan.

About me

Howdy. I've had diverse interests and hobbies throughout life, which led me to analytics in order to leverage the opportunity to be learning new things constantly. When I was young I was obsessed with finding what the strongest or "best" thing in a video game was. What I hadn't realized how much this was like being an analyst, going on the hunt to find what's effective or valuable in any organization, system, or "game" of any kind.

I left a job in late 2021 where I was working in the movie industry as a systems engineer in the post-production mastering space. I found that I'd have a happier future in using data and business intelligence to make an impact in a bigger way than I felt I could as an engineer.

So, with some online resources, I've taught myself how to work with data using Python, SQL, and R. I'd love to use what the skills I've learned to benefit you or your organization. How can we take your game to the next level?

My skills

What can I do for you?

I can gather, clean, analyze, and present findings from any data you have to get the numbers to back the changes you want to make. If I'm not familiar with a tool that's required, I'll learn it.

I've done years of leadership and communications training, and I am confident in my ability to find common ground with anyone to work together effectively.

I'd love to learn about what product or service your organization provides, and how to take a look to find insight together that will save you stress, time, and money - implemented in a manner that the people of your organization buy into and want to see happen.
